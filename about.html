About

Non-governmental organizations (NGOs) strive to address some of the most pressing global issues and promote a better future for all. One of the significant challenges faced by NGOs is to provide education to the uneducated. Furthermore, the existing online platforms come at a cost, which adds an additional hurdle. Therefore, develop a website that offers NGOs a tool to create their websites and a platform to promote their events and activities. Create a solution to assist NGOs in building their online presence and increasing their outreach to raise awareness and garner volunteers to support for their cause.

The website should be able to:

  • Generate webpages for each NGO.
  • Create a landing page for each registered NGO.
  • Showcase an NGO's previous events and display future events.
  • A clean and functional Dashboard to edit the NGO landing page, events and other pages.
  • Enable volunteers to register so that NGO can get a list of interested volunteers and communicate with them for future collaborations.
